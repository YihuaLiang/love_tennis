What is tennis?

Tennis, orginated in England, is a sport played by one against another or a 2 players group against another group with racket. Players use tennis rackets to strike a tennis over or around the net and into the opponent's court. Players would get a point when their opponents fail to play a valid return.

Tennis is an Olympic sport and played by people in all society levels. It is one of the most popular sport in the world now.

There are various kinds of competitions for different levels players. The 4 most popular competitions are called Grand slam: Australian Open, located at Melbourne; French Open located at Paris; Wimbledon located at London; US Open located at New York.

On tennis court, there are sweat but also elegant gentle men and ladies. Come and enjoy tennis.
