I began to play tennis when I was 12. In those summer holiday, I spent 4 hours on tennis everyday.

However, when I gradually grow up, I can't spend so much time on tennis anymore.
Since I have learned skills in IT and computer science, I think I could show my love and experience in tennis in another way.

This website are intended to provide various media resources for tennis lover
no matter whether they play tennis or not and when they start to love tennis.
The stakeholders will find:

For general tennis fans For Beginners For experienced players
Media materials of the great moments in the past grand slam Teaching video for all those skills Test based on NTRP tennis rating system
Those male and female awesome players Basic Rules More advanced skills

I have learned very basic html before, so this project helps me review the skills I have learned.

Furthermore, I learned more useful skills not only in technique coding but also website designments
For example, applying proper tags for different contents is a very significant skill. Controlling white space by padding and
margin is also quite fantastic. The examples provided in JS bin are quite helpful for me to learn what a good website looks like

Since I was the one of the leaders in my home university's tennis club, I will consider adding more contents
relevant to tennis activities on campus. Perhaps I could hand this website to my friend who is the leader now as a template.
Then more interactive contents will be added e.g. registration for the club membership, signing up for a tennis class, booking a court
even a forum to talk about tennis skills. PHP, database or some 3rd party software(wordpress) could also be considered for this website.
