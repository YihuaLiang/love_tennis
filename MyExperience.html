Suggestion on tennis skills

I have been playing tennis for 8 years and learned useful tips from friends.

Don't stand on the court straight but with your knee a little curl.
Begin every actions from your feet and keep your arms and hands relaxed.

Be concentrated on the ball.
run fast and arrive the right position in time. Keep adjusting your body to find the best way to hit the ball
